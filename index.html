Good Mythical Morning -- A YouTube Talk Show

I've been enjoying watching Good Mythical Morning on YouTube. The show also airs a "Show after the show", Good Mythical More. I want to catch that as well. The problem with that is that the 2 shows have their own YouTube Channels. That makes making a playlist of the shows from each channel so you can watch them in order difficult and time consuming. So I am making this website to accomplish the task instead.

I used this tool to download the playlists of the seasons below (already compiled on each channel), and then compiled them in order using some Excel-Fu and YouTube's "Untitled List" feature. Unfortunately, that feature limits playlists to 50 videos, so I have listed the breakouts below. Check out this wiki for more information. I have also made note of where I have noticed missing or out of order shows.
